Cloud


Cloud computing is the on-demand delivery of IT resources over the Internet with pay-as-you-pricing. Instead of buying, owning, and maintaining physical data centers and servers, you can access technologyservices, such as computing power, storage, and databases, on an as-needed basis from a cloud providerlike Amazon Web Services (AWS).



On Permises


On-premise software is installed and runs on a company's own hardware infrastructure, and is hostedlocally, whereas cloud software is stored and managed on the provider's servers, and accessed through web browser or other interface.as much as it has, as its allure and promise offer newfound flexibility for enterprises, everything from saving time and money to improving agility and scalability
